Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 14/11/2024

Campus Cart values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application. By using Campus Cart, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information to create a secure and seamless experience on our app, including:

  • Account Information: We require users to log in using their college email ID ending with '@klu.ac.in' for verification and security.
  • Personal Details: When setting up your profile, we collect your name, phone number, and date of birth (DOB).
  • Product Information: When uploading a product for sale, we collect details about the item, including its description, photos, and category.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data on how you interact with the app, such as browsing patterns, items viewed, and items liked.

2. Information Use

We use your information to:

  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers within the campus.
  • Verify user authenticity for a safe and trusted environment.
  • Improve app functionality, user experience, and security.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Information is shared only with other users in the app for transaction purposes, and only if you engage with them.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

6. User Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account information at any time through the app settings. If you wish to delete your account, please contact our support team.
